Pizza Recipe

ingredients

  • water
  • flour
  • pepperoni
  • cheese of your choice
  • tomato sauce
  • salt
  • olive oil

Steps

  1. preheat oven to 350 degrees
  2. make dough
  3. spread on flat pan after neading
  4. layer tomato sauce
  5. then cheese
  6. then additional toppings
  7. bake pizza for 15 mins while periodically checking on it
  8. let cool
  9. Enjoy!
